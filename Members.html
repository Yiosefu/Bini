About BINI

Filipino pop phenomenon BINI features 8 multi-talented members, Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, Jhoanna, and Sheena. The girls have quickly become the biggest female Filipino artist in history, and second biggest artist overall in the Philippines, holding both the #1 & #2 spots on the top Filipino music charts simultaneously with the hits “Salamin, Salamin” and “Pantropiko.” BINI's sound is shaped by homegrown artists and global sensations alike. On the heels of their unprecedented growth in socials and in streaming listenership (30M +), the girls recently sold out their “BINIverse” tour in record-breaking time, and have been heralded as one of Teen Vogue's 2024 Girl Groups to watch!

Maraiah Queen Arceta (born January 27, 2001) better known as Aiah, is a Filipino rookie performer. She is the eldest member, main rapper & visual of the Filipino girl group BINI



Profile

  • Aiah's favorite color is Black
  • Aiah’s hobbies include swimming and drawing.
  • Her special talent is modelling.
  • Aiah’s favourite SHA subject is dancing.


2001-2018: Early Life

Back in Cebu, Aiah used to join in their school's dance group, performing in school events like Buwan ng Wika , though she didn't venture on learning other dancing styles like hip hop. Outside school, Aiah did modelling and joined beauty pageants[3]. She won a beauty pageant produced by a beauty product company and was hailed as Ms. Silka Philippines 2018. Aiah also appeared in the TV Commercials of these beauty products.



In 2018, she auditioned in the Star Hunt Auditions that were held in Cebu, first auditioning to be casted on ABS-CBN's reality program Pinoy Big Brother.



2019-2020: Star Hunt Academy Trainee

Aiah entered as a trainee in Star Hunt Academy on October 2019, where she was the last to be casted among the girl trainees. Under SHA, she developed her skills in singing and dancing under Filipino voice coaches Kitchy Molina and Anna Graham, and dance coaches Mickey Perz and Matthew Almodovar. She has also undergone the intensive Korean training system with the help of MU Doctor Academy where she learned the Korean practice and discipline in singing and dancing.

Maria Nicolette Vergara (born September 14, 2001) better known as Colet, is a Filipino rookie performer. She is the second eldest member, main vocal, lead dancer & lead rapper of the Filipino girl group BINI.



Profile

  • Colet's favorite color is Black.
  • Colet’s hobbies include singing, dancing and reading books.
  • Her nicknames are Colet, Mani, and Anger.
  • Her special talent is song writing.
  • Colet’s favourite SHA subject is singing.
  • Her favorite Motto is: ”It's okay not to be okay.”
  • Popular line: "Ba't mo natanong?"


2001-2018: Early Life

Back in Bohol, Colet had a normal teen life attending school. Ever since she was a child, she developed a background in singing, thanks to her musician father, who is a vocalist and a drummer. She used to join her school's singing groups and participated in singing competitions to further enhance her vocal skills. She also had a talent in dancing, and used join her school's dance group where they compete in inter-school dance competitions.



In 2018, she joined the Star Hunt Auditions held in Bohol.



2019-2020: Star Hunt Academy Trainee

Colet was one of 250 aspirants to undergo the 5-day boot camp and was selected to become a trainee in Star Hunt Academy early 2019. She almost got removed from the boot camp because of health concerns, but was able to return after she fully recovered. Under SHA, she further developed her skills in singing and dancing under Filipino voice coaches Kitchy Molina and Anna Graham, and dance coaches Mickey Perz and Matthew Almodovar. She also underwent the intensive Korean training system with the help of MU Doctor Academy where she learned the Korean practice and discipline in singing and dancing. She was praised by her Filipino and Korean coaches because of her angelic voice and evident talent in dancing.
